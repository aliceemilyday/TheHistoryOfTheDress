Evening Dress, 1922

This ankle-length evening dress is made of black silk georgette embroidered with golden thread and bugle beads. This evening dress of the early 1920s illustrates the transition between the romantic picture dress with its wide skirt and the more 'Cubist' straight and square shape of the mid 1920s. The stylised embroidered motifs were Chanel's trademark at this period. They witness her relationship with the Russian House of Kitmir, who embroidered exclusively for her.

Design House: House of Chanel
Designer: Gabrielle chanel
Year: 1922
Medium: Silk, glass
Culture: French
Source: Victoria & Albert Museum
