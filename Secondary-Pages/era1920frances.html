Evening Dress & Cape, 1927

Low cut, tubular evening dress worn by Mrs. Post at Eleanor's debut dance at the Ritz Carlton. Dress is of sheer green silk chiffon with narrow shoulder straps and a flared skirt. Decorative mistletoe motifs of bugle beads, clear stones and pearls adorn the neckline, left hip and skirt. The dress is worn with a separate undergarment of pale green silk charmeuse with fine flesh tone lingerie straps. A matching cape is constructed of sheer green silk lined with the silk charmeuse used for the undergarment.

Design House: Frances & Co
Designer: Unknown
Year: 1927
Medium: Silk, beads, fox fur
Culture: American
Source: Hillwood Museum
