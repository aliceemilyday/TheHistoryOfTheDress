Wedding Ensemble, 1925

Woman's Wedding Ensemble by Jeanne Lanvin. The dress is crafted from a silk georgette and tulle with silver lamé appliqué and silk and metallic thread embroidery. The inner slip is made from a silk satin with metallic lace and the headpiece a silver lamé with wax orange blossoms.

Design House: House of Lanvin
Designer: Jeanne Lanvin
Year: 1925
Medium: Silk, tulle
Culture: French
Source: Philadelphia Museum of Art
