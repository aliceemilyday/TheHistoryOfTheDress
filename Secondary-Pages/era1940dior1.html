The History

Day Dress, 1947

Christian Dior launched his couture house in February 1947. Although known for the heavy corsetting and complicated construction of his early collections, not all of Dior's designs relied on complex underpinnings. This lightweight, unlined dress achieves volume through a draped skirt. Dior typically countered the close fitting bodice with Magyar sleeves, which gave ease of movement. The dress has no label and may have been a version of the original by Christian Dior New York, which Dior launched in 1948.

Design House: House of Dior
Designer: Christian Dior
Year: 1947
Medium: Silk twill
Culture: French
Source: Victoria & Albert Museum
